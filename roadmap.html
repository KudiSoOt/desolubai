Roadmap

Phase 1: Launch & Community Growth

Launch Desolubai, host giveaways, and community events. Build a strong presence on social platforms like Twitter and Telegram.

Phase 2: Partnerships & Ecosystem Expansion

Collaborate with other crypto projects for cross-promotion. Introduce staking or holding rewards to incentivize long-term holders.

Phase 3: Advanced Utility Development

Develop and launch a utility feature such as a DeFi tool, meme platform, or marketplace. Release the first version of Desolubai's decentralized application (dApp).

Phase 4: Long-Term Vision & Governance

Establish a community-driven governance system with DAO-style voting. Expand partnerships with established blockchain projects.
